Princeton Gates Millenium Scholars Program


Founded in the Spring of 2013, the Princeton Gates Millennium Scholars Program was established with three main goals in mind: fostering a community on campus between scholars and alumni, giving back to the larger Princeton community by reaching out to high school students and aiding them in the scholarship application process; and promoting greater awareness of the Gates Millennium Scholarship Program's activities to current scholars in the greater Princeton area. As the group has grown, the group seeks to accomplish these fundamental goals, while also interacting and helping to develop the larger low-income, first-generation, and minority community on Princeton's campus.

Princeton Gates Millennium Scholars can be reached using the following email, pugates@princeton.edu.

For more information on the National Gates Millennium Scholars Organization, please refer to: www.gmsp.org
