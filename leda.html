LEDA Scholars at Princeton


LEDA Scholars at Princeton (LSAP) was founded in 2012. The national organization, the Leadership Enterprise for a Diverse America (LEDA) is a national not-for-profit that targets high-achieving low-income juniors in high school and provides them with the necessary resources to attend prestigious and highly selective universities. To learn more information about the national organization, please visit www.ledascholars.org.

To establish a sense of community for scholars on campus. To create mentoring relationships between LEDA Scholars who are currently attending Princeton University in order to ease their transition to college. To reach out to LEDA Scholars who are considering attending Princeton. To raise awareness about the Leadership Enterprise for a Diverse America and its efforts to aid low-income students.

To contact us please e-mail lsap@princeton.edu
