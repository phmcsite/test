Princeton University Quest Scholars Network


QuestBridge is an organization that was founded in 1994 with the goal of connecting high achieving, low income students with top Universities in the US. Once students are accepted by a QuestBridge partner school and elect to go to it, then the student becomes part of the Quest Scholars Network at their college.

The Princeton University Quest Scholars Network was founded in 2012 as an official campus organization. The group exists to provide new Quest Scholars a community of upperclassmen and freshman peers through study breaks and a mentoring program. The group also facilitates discussion on campus climate and issues relating to the social and academic culture of Princeton. To be involved with the group you do not have to be a Quest Scholar; all activities are open to the entire campus and we encourage any interested individuals to participate in the study breaks and dialogue!
